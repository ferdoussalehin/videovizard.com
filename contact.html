Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues affecting service access, we prioritize those first.

I have a technical problem, what should I include?

Please include: device type (computer/phone), browser you're using, what you were trying to do, and any error messages you saw. Screenshots are very helpful!

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase. Email support@stressreleasor.com with your request and we'll process it within 5-10 business days.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently we provide support via email only to ensure thorough, documented assistance. This allows us to provide detailed solutions and keep a record of your support history.
