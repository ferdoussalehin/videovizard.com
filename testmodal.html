🚀 How to Create Your Video

1
Choose how you want to start
Start by selecting how you'd like to create your video:

Script Generation → Use this if you want to create a script from scratch.
You can let AI fully generate the script for you, or guide it with your own inputs.

I Have Content → Choose this if you already have a script ready.
Simply paste your content and it will be automatically split into scenes.

Generate Campaign → Select this if you want to create multiple videos at once.
Ideal for planning content over a specific time period.
2
Set your video preferences
Customize how your video should look and feel.
Choose options like language, reel type, content goal, target audience, tone, and media style.

When you're done, click “Save & Continue”.
3
Provide your video details
Enter the key information needed to generate your script.
This includes your industry, niche, video idea, hook or style, duration, and call-to-action.

Once everything is filled in, continue to generate your script.
4
Review and refine your script
Go through the generated script carefully.
Make any edits or adjustments to ensure it matches your message and style before moving forward.
5
Turn your script into a video
Convert your finalized script into a video automatically with one click.
6
Edit and preview your video
Customize your video by adjusting visuals, audio, and text styles.
Use the preview to see how everything comes together and make final tweaks.
7
Generate your final video
Once you're satisfied, render your video to produce the final version ready for export.
8
Download or publish
Download your completed video or publish it directly to your social media platforms.
